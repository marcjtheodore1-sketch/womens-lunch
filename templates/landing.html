Autistic Women's Lunch

A monthly meetup run by London Autism Group Charity for autistic women to connect over good food

This is a relaxed, neuroaffirming monthly lunch for autistic women aged 18+ run by London Autism Group Charity.

Self-identification is fine — you don't need a formal diagnosis.

Join us for a casual lunch at a traditional London pub. You can choose a main course and one non-alcoholic drink which the charity will be happy to cover. It's a chance to meet other autistic women, share experiences, or simply enjoy a meal in good company.

No pressure to socialise — you can chat as much or as little as you like. There is always at least one charity volunteer onsite to welcome you.

🍽️ What to Expect

  • Monthly lunches
  • 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM
  • Maximum 12 people per lunch
  • Main course and one non-alcoholic drink covered by the charity
  • Relaxed, understanding environment
  • Always at least one charity volunteer onsite
